# Constitutional Commons Protection Clause

## Prevention of Corporate Capture

Notwithstanding the GNU Affero GPL v3, the following restrictions apply:

### No Proprietary Forking
No entity may create proprietary forks or derivatives that would undermine
the constitutional governance purposes of this software.

### No AI Training Without Reciprocation
Large AI companies (defined as entities with >1000 employees or >$1B valuation)
may not use this software for training AI systems without:
- Releasing their improvements under same license
- Contributing significant resources to the commons
- Passing constitutional compliance audits

### Federation Requirement
Any entity using this software at scale must federate with the public network
and cannot create isolated, proprietary instances.

## Enforcement
Violations will be pursued under both copyright law and through public
constitutional governance mechanisms.
