# Human Rights Exception Clause

## Emergency Humanitarian Access

Notwithstanding any other terms, any individual or organization may use, modify, 
and distribute this software without restriction when such use is for:

1. Preventing or mitigating human rights abuses
2. Humanitarian disaster response
3. Public health emergencies
4. Democratic process protection
5. Constitutional governance establishment

## No Reciprocity Required
This exception waives all copyleft requirements for humanitarian uses. 
Attribution is requested but not required in emergency situations.

## Good Faith Standard
Users must reasonably believe their use qualifies under this exception.
